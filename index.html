  • Our Story

    Square began with a simple idea: everyone should be able to accept credit cards. We have been growing with our sellers ever since. We’re building a better point-of-sale and redesigning the payments experience for buyers and sellers alike. We’re building a network of commerce for the benefit of all.

  • Making Payments Personal

    Commerce is the exchange of value, a conversation between buyer and seller. Most financial companies think about the value—we think about the exchange. We create tools that make it easier for people to focus on that conversation and build relationships. We’re designing currency to be a simpler utility and an elegant experience at the same time.

  • We’ve Just Begun

    We want our simpler idea of commerce to empower everyone. We aim to do so by building technology that makes taking payments as simple as plugging in; technologies so pervasive they disappear. While these are simple goals, it is a complex process. There are 7 billion people on the planet. Nearly every one of them exchanges value in some way, every single day. We’ve only just started.
